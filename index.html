Something In The Orange

Zach Bryan plays his song "Something In The Orange" live in concert before a sold-out crowd at The Warfield in San Francisco, California on October 22, 2022. Something In The Orange was released as a single in April 2022 and then appeared on Bryan's third studio album, American Heartbreak (2022).

Burn Burn Burn
